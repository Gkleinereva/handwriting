Hand writing with reveal.js

An extension to write by a hand inside Reveal Js presentations

How to start

Press or toggle on-off of a hand writing mode.

Writing...

  • : on a chalkboard

With the mouse

  • To write: dragging the left mouse button.
  • To erase: pressing , then dragging the left mouse button.

With the pen or touch

  • To write: just touching.
  • To erase: pressing , then touching.

Changing colors

Colour changes each time you press 'c', in which six ones complete in a cycle.
